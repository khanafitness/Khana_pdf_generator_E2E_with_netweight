Instructions
  • Quantity of food items is to be measured cooked except paneer, tofu, besan etc
  • Sabzi quantity mentioned is for cooked sabzi Katoris.
  • Food items can be cooked however you like but with the quantities mentioned.
  • Avoid sugar and excess salt.
  • Multigrain atta/flour can be used but is less preferable.
  • Deep fry food is not allowed.
  • Avoid outside food (cooked in unhealthy conditions, are deep fried and/or cooked with palm oil).
  • Avoid use of plastic containers, bottles.
  • Kindly take print out of these for easy follow.
  • Stress and anxiety need to be completely avoided. Do exercise, breathing techniques to calm nervous system. Stress aggravates hormone imbalance.

Follow below walking plan – Walking needs to be slow and at comfortable pace only. Goal: Reach 8,000–10,000 steps/day lifestyle gradually.
